African Fashion

One cannot talk about African culture without making mention of fashion. Africans love to look good and when presented with the opportunity, they will pull out the best, most-daring outfit they have in their closet.

A typical African fashionista's wardrobe includes a variety of styles, however, most people have a mix between western European and contemporary African pieces.

Some of the popular designers include Italian houses like Gucci and Versace, South African house Rich Mnisi and Nigerian house Orange Culture.

Below are examples of African designers.

The first picture is of Rich Mnisi from South Africa. He draws inspirations from his childhood tocreate clothes

The second is of Orange Culture from Nigeria. This brand is all about color blocking and loose-type western-inspired clothing.

The last one is of Emmy Kasbit from Nigeria. This brand fuses ancient textile-production with western formal silhouettes. Emmy Kasbit primarily makes suits with an African twist.
